SRM

EVENTS

SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST) hosts a wide array of events that enhance campus life and provide students with opportunities to engage, learn, and showcase their talents. Key events include technical fests like Aaruush and Milan, where students from engineering and technology backgrounds participate in workshops, seminars, hackathons, and competitions to solve real-world problems. Cultural festivals celebrate diverse arts through music, dance, and drama competitions, while sports events promote fitness and sportsmanship. The university also organizes national and international conferences and seminars, providing students access to expert insights and industry trends. Workshops and training sessions on coding, entrepreneurship, and soft skills keep students updated with the latest developments. Additionally, SRM is committed to social and environmental initiatives, conducting events like blood donation camps and tree plantation drives. These events contribute to the holistic development of students by allowing them to explore interests, develop skills, and connect with peers and professionals globally.
